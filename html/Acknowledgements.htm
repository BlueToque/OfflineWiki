Acknowledgements

TrueNorth is the result of over four years of work on the part of a single programmer, but it by no means is the result of the efforts of a single person. While I have contributed the labour and the expertise, without the support of my friends and family, this program would not have been possible.

Most of the core ideas for TrueNorth came from the many discussions and complaints I have heard from Search and Rescue volunteers, myself included, before, during and after searches. It's the worst thing in the world to be asked to search for a missing person in the middle of a cold rainy night, but being given a terrible map makes the experience frustrating, dangerous, and sometimes futile. From this inspiration, this project was born; that SAR members should have access to professional quality maps and never have to go into the field with a photocopied air photo again.

Over the years I've worked on this project, I have taken input from experts in the SAR field whose experience far outstrips my own, and incorporated those ideas into the software. Things like simple printing, easy coordinate entry, and keeping it extremely simple were the major points, and these you can see in the finished product.

Because this project was started to ease the pain of SAR members, and because of their input, I dedicate this project to you; your dedication and courage saves the lives of people every day. I hope this tool makes your job easier.
